Cycling Groups Bot is a Telegram chatbot designed to help you find cycling groups in your area. It serves as a comprehensive catalog of cycling groups, making it easy for you to connect with fellow cyclists and never ride alone again.

The chatbot assists you in finding cycling groups near you. By joining these groups, you can cycle more frequently, enhance your training, and make new friends along the way.

Our curators carefully select and verify registered cycling groups, adding only those known and trusted by reliable sources. However, it's still important for users to reach out to local bicycle shops and consult the community for additional assurance.

Using the chatbot is completely free of charge.

Contact chatbot administrator for more information: cycling-groups-bot@diegoqueres.net.
